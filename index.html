Type of Camera
TypeElectronically controlled 35mm focal plane shutter, single-lens reflex AE camera
Photography systemProgram AE, aperture priority AE, and manual photography
Standard lensMD 50mm F1.2  /  MD 50mm F1.4  /  MD 50mm F1.7
Lens mountMinolta SLR bayonet mount
FilmJIS 135 rolled film
Image field24mm × 36mm
Shutter
TypeElectronically controlled focal plane shutter, traveling horizontally
Shutter speeds Auto (P and A modes): 4 sec. to 1/1000 sec. (stepless)
Manual: 1, 1/2, 1/4, 1/8, 1/15, 1/30, 1/60, 1/125, 1/250, 1/500, 1/1000 sec. and B (bulb)
Shutter dialClick stop endless dial with P/A lock position
Shutter release Electromagnetic release. Remote cord and wireless controller IR-1 compatible.
Locks in case of battery voltage drop.
Sensor switch keeps metering active for 15 sec. after touch.
Intermittent alarm when indicated shutter speed falls below 1/30 sec. (main switch ON).
Self-timer Electronic, 10 sec. delay. LED blink + intermittent alarm indication. Pre-operation notice given. Can be cancelled after start.
Synchro / Flash
Flash syncX contact — electroflash sync at 1/60 sec. and slower. Flash bulb at 1/15 sec. and slower.
Hot shoeDirect contact with electric shock prevention device, synchro auto control contact
Synchro terminalJIS B type socket
Film Winding & Rewinding
Film windingSingle-operation lever, 130° winding angle, 30° preliminary angle. Compatible with Motor Drive 1 and Auto Winder G.
Film counterAuto resetting, additive calculation
Film rewindingAuto rewind and crank system. Auto reset of rewind button.

MirrorSlide-up quick return

Note
This Parts List is based on existing models as of January 1983 (2017-200 Black model).

Parts modified during production are marked on exploded view diagrams with the following symbols:

● Modified during production. Not individually interchangeable with previous type. (Some parts may be interchangeable — refer to specified page listed under part number.)
◎ Discontinued during production — newly added or temporarily used part. (Refer to specified page listed under part number.)
Important
For modification details, see P. 21 or later. Read and understand P. 21 before proceeding with parts identification.

Model coverage notes:

AE lock variants This Parts List is based on the X-700 with AE lock. Two variants exist on the market: AE lock and non-AE lock. For X-700 without AE lock, refer to P. 35–40 for exclusive parts.
2017-100 Chrome model Chrome-exclusive parts described on P. 20. All other parts are common with 2017-200 — refer to P. 1–17. For flexible P.C. board wiring of 2017-100, refer to P. 39–40 (AE lock not provided on Chrome model).
